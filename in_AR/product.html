Augmented Reality is an amazing technology to improve the shopping experience for your customers. With our inAR application you can bring the in-store experience to everyone’s home.

inAR gives you the opportunity to provide professional designer advice to your clients.

It also naturally encourages your clients to make more purchases. Customer hesitation is greatly reduced as one of the significant barriers to making that purchase decision is removed. Customers no longer need to rely on their own imagination as to how a piece will “look” in situ. They have the confidence to make more purchases, more quickly as the need for their own visualisation is reduced.

Of course this also reduces the number of post-sale returns for those with a lesser ability to imagine an object in their home surroundings.

inAR increases the social presence of your furniture brand thanks to the native sharing function.

Now your customers can share all your products, making best use of “user generated content marketing” showcasing all their “own designs” on Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp and many others.

“Information is Power” - inAR allows you to collect and analyse statistics, identifying the level of customer appreciation of each product and other metrics concerning customer behaviour.

InAR is an app designed for almost all IOS and Android devices, not only for the latest versions of iPhone and Android.

Integrate the inAR app into your physical and online store, extend the reach of print media and remove the language barrier with our UI design.

How many customers would turn down the opportunity to ” Try all the furniture at home before you buy it!”

Last but not least. Be unique in a crowded marketplace!
